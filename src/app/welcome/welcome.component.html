What can we do for you?

Here at K9Catch we are here to provide a unique service for people to search for companions for their beloved dogs as well as potential breeding matches.

We also run regular socialisation events enabling dogs and owners to make new friends! By joining us you will have the facilities to do all of this and be part of the friendly K9Catch community!

We also have a for sale page allowing members to purchase fantastic merchandise.
